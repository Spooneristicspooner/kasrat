IDEA:

 

•35 minute film with relevant music and infographics on ‘How to stay fit and live a healthy life’ to develop healthy lifestyle habits in adolescents that lead to optimum mental stability, fitness and eventually wealth.

 

•Target Group: 12-15 years, municipality school students.

 

•Stressing on the importance ‘Health is Wealth’ through the exercise regime and development of healthy eating habits.

 

•Real and long term benefits of regular exercise and living a healthy lifestyle.

 

•The film has Mr. Jackky Bhagnani demonstrating these exercise workflows while giving examples from his own life the disadvantages of leading an unhealthy lifestyle and how he personally turned things over in his life by just adopting a healthier lifestyle. For eg – How he went from 140 kilos in high school to the extremely well-toned actor he is now, today.

 

The video:

 

•Jackky at multiple location (2-3) demonstrating the exercise workflow and tips, tricks and trivia from a professional actor that he is now.

 

•Video to be shot both in interiors and exteriors (magic hour).

 

•The final flow of the video will depend largely on the exercise workflow and script. The film would be a combination of aesthetically shot video of Jackky demonstrating the exercise work flow at these locations supported by infographics of calori monitor to highlight the importance of cheap, healthy food an eating habits. For eg, - Replacing vadapav with a banana, having sprouts for breakfast etc.

 

•Dance workout sequences during ‘cool down’ to keep the target audience engaged in the video.

 

•(Optional) – Ending the video with testimonials of living a healthy lifestyle by prominent actors and celebrities such as John Abraham, Tiger Shroff, Varun Dhawan, Siddharth Mahotra, Alia Bhatt, M.S. Dhoni, Virat Kohli etc.
